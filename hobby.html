fritzie's HOBBIES

Fritzie's hobbies include Pilates and reading.

Reading

Fritzie loves to read and typically reads over 50 books every year. Her favorite book genre is romantasy, which is a blend of romance and fantasy. Her favorite author is the "Blood and Ash" series writer, Jennifer L. Armentrout. She finds it hard to choose a favorite book. Still, some of her top fiction recommendations are "When the Moon Hatched" by Sarah A. Parker and "The House in the Cerulean Sea" by TK Klune. She also loves the biography about Ruth Bader Ginsburg called "The Notorious RBG: The Life and Times of Ruth Bader Ginsburg".

Pilates

When Fritzie isn't enjoying a good book, you might find her at her favorite Pilates studio, Club Pilates! Pilates is a low-impact exercise method developed by Joseph Pilates in the early 20th century to strengthen the body and mind. It focuses on core strength, flexibility, and mindful movement. Through controlled exercises and breathwork, Pilates enhances posture, balance, and overall well-being for all fitness levels.
